Welcome !

This is a template for a simple marketing or informational website. It includes a large callout called a jumbotron and three supporting pieces of content. Use it as a starting point to create something more unique.

But first... what is Mad Libs?

From Wikipedia... "Mad Libs is a phrasal template word game where one player prompts others for a list of words to substitute for blanks in a story, before reading the – often comical or nonsensical – story aloud". In other words, you fill in blanks and then those words form part of a story!
